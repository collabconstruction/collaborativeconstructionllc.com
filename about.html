About Collaborative Construction

Wesleigh Behn

Owner & Founder

Collaborative Construction was founded by Wesleigh Behn, a Massachusetts native with over 30 years of experience in both residential and commercial construction. Wesleigh holds a Bachelor's degree from Boston University and completed postgraduate studies at the Boston Architectural Center.

He has maintained an active CSL license since 2000 and brings deep expertise in all phases of building. He is committed to building Collaborative Construction into a trusted name for high-quality construction across Cape Cod and the South Shore.
